Tempomat is a macOS menu bar app that constantly monitors your CI pipelines.

  • Runs in the background, always available.
  • Notifies when a branch fails to build.
  • Trigger rebuilds right from the app.
  • Detailed information without the need to open the browser.

*Currently only CircleCI is supported, but more CI systems are coming!
